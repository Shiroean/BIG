Ownership and Leadership of Binance

Founder and CEO: Binance was founded by Changpeng Zhao (CZ) in July 2017. Zhao is a well-known figure in the cryptocurrency industry and has played a significant role in Binance's growth and success. Prior to founding Binance, Zhao worked at various financial and technology companies, including Bloomberg and OKCoin.

Leadership Team: Binance's leadership team includes several experienced professionals with backgrounds in finance, technology, and blockchain. The team is responsible for overseeing the platform's operations, development, and strategic direction. Binance has also hired experts in regulatory compliance and security to address the challenges of operating in a rapidly evolving industry.

Advisors and Partners: Binance has collaborated with various advisors and partners to support its growth and development. This includes partnerships with blockchain projects, financial institutions, and technology providers. The platform has also engaged with industry experts to enhance its offerings and stay ahead in the competitive market.
